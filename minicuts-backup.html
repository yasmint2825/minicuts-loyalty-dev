🌐 How to change Downloads folder in your browser
🟡 Google Chrome ▼
1
Click the three dots ⋮ menu (top right) → Settings
2
Search for Downloads in the settings search bar
3
Click Change next to the current download location
4
Navigate to your desired folder (e.g. Google Drive sync folder\MiniCuts Backups) and click Select Folder
5
Optionally turn OFF "Ask where to save each file" so it saves automatically every time
🔵 Microsoft Edge ▼
1
Click ⋯ Settings and more (top right) → Settings
2
Go to Downloads from the left menu
3
Click Change next to Location and pick your folder
4
Turn off "Ask me what to do with each download" for automatic saving
🦊 Mozilla Firefox ▼
1
Click ☰ Menu → Settings
2
Under Files and Applications → Downloads
3
Select "Save files to" and click Browse to pick your folder
🧭 Safari (Mac) ▼
1
Click Safari menu → Settings (or ⌘,)
2
Go to General tab
3
Change "File download location" to your desired folder
💡 Pro tip: Set your download folder to a Google Drive or OneDrive sync folder. Every backup will automatically upload to the cloud within seconds — zero extra effort.
