    The Science
    Why Hair Does What It Does
    💧
    Moisture = Elasticity
    Hydrated hair bends without breaking. Dry hair snaps more easily under combing.
    🧂
    Scalp Sebum is Good
    Natural oils protect the scalp. Over-washing removes them and causes dryness.
    🌡️
    Heat Weakens Bonds
    Hot water and blow dryers weaken keratin bonds. A cool rinse seals the cuticle.
    ✂️
    Split Ends Travel Up
    Untreated split ends split further up the shaft. Regular trims stop the damage.
